EigenCodec: Ultra-Low Bitrate Narrowband Speech Coding via Eigen-Informed Subspace Projection and Multi-Scale Depthwise Aggregation

This page provides supplementary audio samples for our submitted manuscript to JASA.

Fairness Protocol: We present audio samples here at 0.8 kbps from the LibriSpeech dataset as a representative case. To ensure a strictly fair comparison, All end-to-end neural baselines (EnCodec, HiFi-Codec, MSCACodec, DAC, DAC-s, HILCodec) were explicitlyretrained from scratch on the 8 kHz LibriSpeech dataset and constrained to an identical bottleneck (25 Hz frame rate × 4 codebooks) to ensure a strictly fair comparison.
